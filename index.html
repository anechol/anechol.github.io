Who I Am

Howdy! I'm a creatively ambitious individual who graduated from Illinois State University with a B.S. in Psychology. What? Psychology? It's true! During my junior year of college, I realized my heart wasn't really in Psychology. In summer of 2014, I decided to learn coding as a side project to make blog themes. After graduation, I dabbled in code until I realized how much I enjoyed mixing technology and art. And thus, the developer in me was born!

Aside from learning code, my biggest hobby is gaming. I consider myself a huge video game nerd as I've been playing since I was about 3. I plan on studying programming and game development in my spare time. I also enjoy writing fiction and practicing art using Photoshop and Blender.


What I Do

I'm a web developer who specializes in the front end. I prefer smooth, clean work, which is reflected in the projects I've completed. Due to past jobs held, I'm comfortable working in group-oriented environments and enjoy learning from others. As a developer and creative mind, I believe the best quality anyone can have is a desire to learn more than they knew yesterday.

As a self-taught dev, I've learned a handful of technologies that I use to build projects from the ground up.

Languages: HTML5, CSS3 (Sass), Javascript (jQuery)

Web Dev Tools: Atom, Brackets, Bootstrap, Command Line Interface (Cygwin), Git, GitHub, GitKraken, Gulp, npm, Windows (7, 10)
